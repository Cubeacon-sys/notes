\section{Translating Propositional Logic Statements}
\begin{enumerate}
	\item Identify atomic propositions
	\item Determine apporopriate logical connectives
\end{enumerate}
If i go to the store of The movies, i won't do my homework.\\
$ p $: I go to the store \\
$ q $: I go to the movies \\
$ r $: I do my homework \\


$(p \lor q) \implies \neg r$

\subsection{Practice}
\begin{enumerate}
	\item You can get a free sandwich on thursday if you buy a sandwich or a cup of soup.
	\item You can get a free sandwich on thursday only if you buy a sandwich or a cup of soup.
	\item The automated reply can't be sent when the system is full.

\end{enumerate}
\textbf{Solution:}
\begin{enumerate}
	\item $ p $: You can get a free sandwich on thursday \\
		$ q $: You buy a sandwich \\
		$ r $: You buy a cup of soup \\
		$ (q \lor r) \implies p $
	\item $ p $: You can get a free sandwich on thursday \\
		$ q $: You buy a sandwich \\
		$ r $: You buy a cup of soup \\
		$ p \implies (q \lor r) $
	\item $ p $: The automated reply can be sent \\
		$ \neg p $: The automated reply can't be sent \\
		$ q $: The system is full \\
		$ q \implies \neg p $
\end{enumerate}
\subsection{Translating Propositions}

\textbf{Translate}: $ (r \lor \neg s) \implies \neg q $ \\


\textbf{Solution}: (if $ r $ \textbf{or} not $ s $, \textbf{then} not $ q $) \\
